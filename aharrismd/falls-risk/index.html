      Design principles behind this calculator

      • Setting-specific baseline risk rather than one score for everyone.
      • Combines sentinel events, functional tests, medical drivers, medications, and environment.
      • Shows uncertainty when key measurements are missing.
      • Returns prevention actions instead of just a label.

      Evidence Notes

      Open research summary

      The referenced 2016 inpatient comparison found limited accuracy for legacy tools, with STRATIFY performing best but only around 63% overall accuracy in that cohort. This prototype deliberately moves away from a single imported cut point toward a layered assessment model that can later be trained and calibrated against public datasets.
